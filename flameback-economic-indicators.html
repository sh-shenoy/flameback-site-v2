How to read this page.

Each tile shows the current value, the change from the previous reported period, and a 12-month trend. The colour of the arrow reflects directional change in the indicator itself, not interpretation — rising inflation is “up” but is generally an unwelcome direction. Sources are listed below each tile and link to the official series. Refresh cadences vary: weekly for FX reserves, monthly for PMI/CPI/IIP, quarterly for GDP, on-announcement for the repo rate.
